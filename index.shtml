About Me

I’ve always loved travelling. No wonder that I ended up studying 2000km away from home – I still remember the day when my plane has first landed in Birmingham on September 22, 2010.

This is when my story as an IT geek began.

I open my own doors. When my peers look for a safe option, I search for challenge. When my friends went to universities back home in Lithuania, I travelled to study to UK, aged nineteen. I arrived without knowing anyone, with limited funds and made it work. By the end of my first year I got a job in a small town with limited employment opportunities, became financially independent from my parents and achieved consistent marks of 60% upwards at University for the assignments and exams.

At the moment I am a Computer Science finalist at Aberystwyth University on track for a 2:1 degree with genuine passion for hardware and proven troubleshooting skills. I returned back to my studies in September after an Industrial Year that I have spent as an ICT technician in Information Services department of Aberystwyth University.

My final year at University will soon come to an end and it will be the time to open a new door...
